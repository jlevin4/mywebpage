Teacher



What do teachers do?

A teacher is someone in this position teaches kids, teenagers, or college students different subjects in school. Depending on the subject, teachers will give out tests, homework, and projects for the students to do to show what they have learned. These assignments are graded in a way to show if the students actually learned the material presented or not.


Average Salary Education Needed Main Skills
About $66,397 per Year To teach in Illinois, teachers must have a teaching license and a bachelor’s degree. Teachers also need to be knowledgeable about the subject they are teaching. The most important qualities of a good teacher are communication, listening, patience, empathy, collaboration, and adaptability.

Why do I want to be a teacher?

I want to be a teacher because I want help teach kids in a way that will be useful to make them successful later in life.

Do I see myself working this job in the future?

This is a career that I can see myself working in the future because I am confident in myself that I will continue to do well in school to learn and do well enough to get a teaching permit and bachelor’s degree.
