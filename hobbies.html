Sports



One of my hobbies is playing sports. When I was younger I played a lot of different sports such as soccer, cross country, track, basketball, volleyball, and baseball. Today I still play baseball and I'm planning on playing either volleyball or track. In high school I also started playing football and bowling.

Video Games



Another one of my hobbies is playing video games. I don't play as much as I used to when I was younger. The games I would play were mainly Nintendo games such as Mariokart and Super Smash Bros. Now I don't have much time to play and if I do play it would just be on my phone.

Hanging Out with Friends



My final hobby is hanging out with my friends. Whenever we would hang out, we would just ride around the neighborhood on our bikes. We usually stop at a park to play some kind of sport like wiffle ball or tennis. Otherwise we would go to someone's house to hang out. At their house we would either go swimming or play video games.
